Paddings, margins and borders are CSS properties that are used to arrange/place the elements. There is the box model that contains the content,padding,border and margin. Padding is the space around the content of the element that seperates it from the border of that element. Border is then between the margin and padding. I like to say border is the box around the content while the padding is the space around that content. Margin is then the outer lining of the element and is found between the border and the margin of a neighbouring element.

Understanding how padding, border and margin works can help to place elements properly on a web page. Hope you enjoyed reading my POV of these three. BYE!!!
